##  Project Overview

This project presents an advanced **Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard** designed to evaluate:

- Sales and profit performance
- Regional and state-wise sales distribution
- Segment-level contribution
- Product category profitability
- Time-based profit trends

The dashboard supports dynamic filtering by:
- Region (Central, East, South, West)
- Ship Mode
- Year (2014–2017)

---

##  Key Business Insights

###  Challenges
- Low profit margin in Furniture category
- Profit volatility across months
- Dependency on Consumer segment

---

###  Opportunities
- Improve pricing strategy for Furniture
- Expand Technology product offerings
- Optimize regional sales strategy

---

###  Recommendations
- Focus on high-margin products (Technology)
- Reduce discount dependency
- Improve cost management in Furniture
- Strengthen underperforming segments
